# Predictive AI

Predictive AI is a capability of SAP AI Core that handles large volumes of data and offers scalable machine learning capabilities within a governed, enterprise-ready runtime environment.

SAP AI Core provides the runtime and lifecycle management foundation for AI scenarios on SAP Business Technology Platform \(SAP BTP\). Within this foundation, predictive AI allows you to build, deploy, and manage predictive models and machine learning pipelines while leveraging the security, scalability, and operational controls of SAP AI Core.

With predictive AI, you can:

-   Access supported predictive models
-   Train and deploy machine learning pipelines
-   Apply forecasting and classification to business data
-   Integrate predictive capabilities into business applications
-   Operate predictive AI workloads under enterprise governance

All model execution, resource management, and operational control are handled by SAP AI Core.
